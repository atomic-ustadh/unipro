Meet the H.O.D of Theatre Arts

Mr. HOD of Theatre Arts

Overview

The B.A. Theatre Arts Programme is designed to produce graduates that will be well grounded in the theory and practice of theatre arts. It is expected that students will be adequately exposed to drama movements and performance techniques, philosophies and worldviews that that have sustained the practice of Theatre Arts through the ages. The programme aims at turning out graduates who will adequately fit into media sector, Public Relations, Art Councils, Theatre Scholarship and professional theatre practice.
